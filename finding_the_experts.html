Dr. Maria Stefania Cataleta

FSW Library Source

    This is Dr. Maria Stefania Cataleta. She is an Italian lawyer and lecturer in several universities spanning Italy and France. She received her Ph.D. in Public Law from the Université Côte d'Azur in France and her master degree in international law (LL.M.) from the City University of London in the UK (“Short Biographies”). In regard to the article, Cataleta is expressing her ethical concerns regarding AI and speaks about the legal ramifications of not keeping the technology in check.

Sara Brown

Online Source

    This is Sara Brown. She has her master’s degree in journalism from Boston University and undergraduate from the University of Washington. Her article summarizes the findings found in a MIT publication made by one of MIT’s professor of economics Daron Acemoglu and Boston University associate professor of economics Pascual Restrepo. Both professors have experience in research and, in their paper, wanted to find how much industrial robotics affected jobs and wages.
