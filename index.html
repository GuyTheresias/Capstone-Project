About Me

   Hello, my name is Guy Theresias, pronounced "Gee" in French. I have a passion for creativity and enjoy hobbies that allow me to express it, such as thrifting and sewing. By combining these interests, I can transform thrifted items into something uniquely my own, whether by adding personal touches or repairing imperfections. This blend of sustainability and craftsmanship is something I truly value.

   Academically, I’m drawn to robotics with a focus on integrating artificial intelligence. My research interests include exploring how AI can enhance robotic systems to address real-world challenges in fields like healthcare, manufacturing, and environmental sustainability. I am fascinated by the potential of combining advanced technology with human-centric solutions to create a meaningful impact.

   Music is a constant source of inspiration and joy in my life. I enjoy exploring diverse genres, including rap, R&B, indie, pop, and grunge, and I’m always open to discovering something new. There’s something uniquely uplifting about finding a song or artist that resonates, and I believe music is a powerful tool for boosting positivity.

Why Am I Here?

   I’m here because of my passion for robotics and artificial intelligence. My research focuses on how AI can enhance robotic systems to solve real-world challenges, from improving healthcare to advancing manufacturing and promoting environmental sustainability.

   I’m fascinated by the potential to merge cutting-edge technology with human-centric solutions to create meaningful change. Being here allows me to deepen my understanding of artificial intelligence while exploring the ethical considerations that guide its responsible application in robotics. This knowledge is essential for achieving my goal of developing innovative, impactful systems.
